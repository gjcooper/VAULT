## My Thoughts

 A very high level overview of sequential sampling models in general as well as the [[diffusion decision model|drift diffusion model]] specifically. It gives a brief look at the history of these models, a description of the main parameters of the DDM and a cursory look at alternative models. Perhaps useful as a primer for people ubnfamiliar with the DDM.

It also includes a quickfire coverage of the different applications of the model in various areas such a [[value-based judgements]], clinical and ageing research and more. There is also a focus towards the end on the applications in conjunction with neuroscience methods such as [[single- cell recordings]] and [[EEG]] for instance.

This section on neuroscience is ever so slightly more in depth as it covers some extensions of the model or variations of the model that are more neurobiologically informed.
