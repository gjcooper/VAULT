## My Thoughts

An overview of the many issues with [[peer review]] within academia at the moment. I do like how the many possible problems and/or solutions provided by the increased use of AI and specifically [[large language models]] was *not* the focus of this piece. Rather the more human aspects of peer review took front of stage.

There were a few possible or in progress attempts to "solve" the isues with peer review, however this manuscript does not intend to solve the issue (or detail how it is being solved). Instead it acts as a flag to call attention to the authors perceived prominent issues and perhaps act as a call to action for new minds to challenge the status quo.
