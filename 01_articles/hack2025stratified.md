## My Thoughts

A study on [[precision medicine]] approaches to the treatment of [[major depressive disorder]]. Patients with a [[cognitive control]] subtype of [[major depressive disorder|MDD]] were given an $\alpha_{2A}$ [[receptor agonist]] and showed a better than standard practice response in terms of a reduction in depressive symptoms as measured by the 17-item [[Hamilton Depression Rating Scale]].

The way the "subtype" of MDD was created was through [[fMRI]] analysis on a [[cognitive control]] task ([[Go-NoGo]]). This is relevant to the goals of [[Hypatia Health]], as we can hopefully offer a more cost-effective alternative to mass [[fMRI]]
