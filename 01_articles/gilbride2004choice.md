## My Thoughts

How have I not read this before - here they try to dfistinguish between multiple choice screening strategies in the creation of [[consideration sets]], combined with the subsequent final choice using more traditional [[Random Utility Model]] methods. The choice set strategies investigated here include the [[Conjunctive Strategy]], the [[Disjunctive Strategy]] and a [[compensatory]] strategy.

This would be a valuable new resource for my [[Architecture of MultiAttribute Choice]] paper under current review.
