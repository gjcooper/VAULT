## My Thoughts

An interesting overview of the specific problems related to the uptake of cognitive science research within the country of Nigeria. Some additional calls to action to increase the involvement of the global majority in cognitive science research as well.

The paper focused on three aspects of the context within Nigeria, including access to tools (either by cost or non-local tools), access to participants reduced either due to a disinterest (or unfamiliarity) with cognitive research - or misapprehensions due to religiosity or cultural background. This lack of awareness is also a factor in the lack of researchers from Nigeria being drawn to cognitive science research, along with limited access to training and funding.
