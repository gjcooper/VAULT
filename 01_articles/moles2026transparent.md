## My Thoughts

While this is an interesting idea I think it is very difficult or maybe even impossible in practice to implement.

For a start the logistical hurdles necessary to overcome to get the majority of reputable publishing players onboard are significant, with coordination with ORCid, new processes etc all a problem without a current solution.

Additionally the system looks like it will disadvantage academics from outside the mainstream western university systems given the already current difficulties accessing the big publishers.

Although listed in the limitations, I think the authors downplay the downsides. Buying authorship already happens - this is another incentive to do so, and they also handwave away the risk of AI reviews.

I think a first step would be an easily visible/shareable system for displaying how many reviews (recent reviews?) an academic has could create a low key reputational incentive - certainly for younger researchers.
