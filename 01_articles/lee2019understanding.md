## My Thoughts

This takes the work in [[lee2016bayesian|Bayesian outcome-based strategy classification]] and extends it even further by considering two main extra facets.

For one it extends the graphical models as implemented in JAGS etc to consider (and in some cases account for) new behavioural data. Specifically the models are augmented with both search data (via MouseLab style revealing of attribute values) and with verbal report data that was classified (with high interrate reliability)

Secondly the final model additionally incorporates a term to capture switching between the various component models. (These models include the [[Take the Best]], a [[Tallying]] strategy and a [[Weighted Additive Rule]])

There are interesting differences between the highlighted strategies depending on whether additional sources of behavioural data are considered - and whether switching is incorporated. I would like to see a greater illustration of model fits.
