## My Thoughts

This piece is a little on the old side - but it does have some interestintg nuggets. The "evidence accumulation" here was a little bit of a side track, as it is not a standard model, but as each cue is potentially integrated into the choice it accumulates evidence for or against on of the option.

It is an early example of integrating the [[Take the Best]] with a rational model (perhaps something like the [[Weighted Additive Rule]]) which is nice.

In fact one framing of my work with the [[Architecture of MultiAttribute Choice]] paper is that each of the "steps" in the random walk model of eviidence accumulation here can be thought of as perhaps like one of my [[elementary decision process]] steps.
