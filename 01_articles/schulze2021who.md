## My Thoughts

This introduces (to me) the concept of [[social sampling]]. That is the idea that when trying to assess the rates of something you may sample your social circles (family, friends, acquaintances) to figure out what the actual rate may be.

The authors compare their social sampling model to other heuristics and random guessing using a [[latent-mixture classification parameter]]. This has some relevance for my [[Architecture of MultiAttribute Choice]] paper.
