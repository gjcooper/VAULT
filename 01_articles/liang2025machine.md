## My Thoughts


I like framing the of hallucination problem in this different way of producers of bullshit.

One aspect they introduce is a technique of calculating a bullshit index, the difference between the internal agents belief (as measured by their one word agreement on truth statements) and the rating of their explicit statements on the initial prompt.

Another new aspect to this work is the introduction of types of bullshit in the written word, aspects of bullshitting like “weasel words”, “empty rhetoric” and “paltering”..

Some relationship here to the [[hicks2024chatgpt|ChatGPT is bullshit]] paper, and some of the excellent youtube series on bullshit: [UQ iSchool series on bullshit in the age of big data](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLPnZfvKID1Sje5jWxt-4CSZD7bUI4gSPS&si=LgIRfmRx6aQd08cs)
