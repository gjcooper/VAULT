## My Thoughts

A precursor to [[Lei Zhang]]'s and others work on the [[reinforcement learning]] + [[diffusion decision model|drift diffusion model]] work in prosocial behaviour, al la -> [[kutlikova2023testosterone|Testosterone eliminates strategic prosocial behavior through impacting choice consistency in healthy males]].

Some things to like here are the comparisons of multiple RLDDM models to separate RL and DDM models of varying complexity. [[Laura Fontanesi]] et al also compare their model to another RLDDM model proposed by [[Mads Lund Pedersen]], [[Michael J Frank]] and [[Guido Biele]] in 2017.

There are some nice notes on how to represent the models in a diagrammatic way in the appendices - and particularly code (hopefully for [[stan]]) is available from a Python package (see https://rlssm.readthedocs.io/en/latest/simulations/simulateDDM.html).
